Our Missions

We work for the patients’ well being and we promise that
We will give the best treatment at all times to give relief from pain and suffering.
We will work enthusiastically and sincerely to save life, because it is precious to ALL of us.
We will keep the patients and their relatives satisfied at all times.
We will perform this “Noble Duty” with a smile.
We in the “Non-Treatment Departments” will form an excellent team with “Medical People” to achieve the above goals.
We will continuously educate ourselves, our colleagues and our community regarding diseases, health and preventive medicine.
